### Body (4 paragraphs, italic body font)

Hi, I'm Jo. I bake custom cupcakes out of my home kitchen in Austin for kids' birthdays — the kind where your child gets to pick something wildly specific (a princess rainbow kitten, a roaring dinosaur, a fairy on a mushroom) and actually get it.

It started when I made cupcakes for my niece's birthday and every parent at the party wanted to know if I'd do theirs next. So here we are.

I keep things small on purpose: vanilla or chocolate, your colors, your toppers, made fresh to order. My mom turned every one of my birthdays into a whole production growing up — this is me doing a version of that for someone else's kid.

Tell me what your kid is dreaming up. I'll bake it.

## FAQ page (`/faq`)

### Title
The fine print (we'll keep it short)

### Q&A

**How far ahead do I need to order?**
72 hours for 6–18 cupcakes. A full week for 24 or 36. The earlier the better, especially on weekends.

**Do you deliver?**
Yes — anywhere within Austin ISD boundaries for a flat $10. Pickup is free.

**Where's pickup?**
You'll get the address when Jo writes back to confirm.

**How do I pay?**
Venmo, Zelle, or cash at pickup or delivery. No deposit needed for orders under $80; larger orders may require a 50% deposit, which Jo will mention when confirming.

**Can I cancel or change my order?**
Yes, up to 48 hours before pickup. Within 48 hours, changes depend on what's already been bought or baked.

**What about allergies?**
The kitchen handles wheat, dairy, and eggs, and is not nut-free. Always tell Jo about allergies in the notes — she'll let you know what's possible.

**Are the cake toppers edible?**
Most are printed cardstock toppers on food-safe sticks (not edible). Edible glitter and sprinkles are food-grade. Tell Jo if you need everything edible (for a smash cake, etc.).

**Can I get more than 36?**
Email jo@joscupcakes.com — large orders are possible with extra lead time.

**Do you do flavors besides vanilla and chocolate?**
Not yet. Maybe later.
